Cycle training is for ages 5 through 70. Road bicycle consists of crank set having a 52 tooth ring gear and a 39 tooth ring gear. The road bicycle is the best place to start for juniors and mens or womens up to the age of 50. Cyclist need their bicycle fitted in order to get the proper size of bike frame. For both men and women. Bicycle shops are the best place to start. By asking a bike shop to fit you. To a bicycle frame by appointment or walk in visit.

Before you ride off

Cyclist should check their bikes components for wear and tear before riding. Looking close at components such as pedals, crank arms, sprocket, shifters, tires, spokes, and wheel locks.
